• You need a friend / peer to play this game
  • Take the help of your friend and ask him/her to control the enemy in each round
  • The enemy cannot move or jump but it sure can attack. To attack with the enemy character you have to click DownArrow/PgDn button
  • Ask your friend to operate this button such that whenever you come near to kill the enemy, the enemy will put on a tough fight
  • Use the keys "A" and "D" to move left and right respectively
  • Press the key "W" to jump. Press "W" twice to double jump
  • Press "Space" to attack the enemy
  • Attack and kill the enemy in the given time to clear and move to next level
  • If the timer runs out and the enemy is still alive then you will lose and have to start the game again from the first level
  • You die when your health bar reaches 0
  • Once reduced, you can't increase your health in that specific level until you finish it. So, be careful around the enemy
  • Your health will refill to maximum after each level irrespective of how much you have in your previous level
  • Score is calculated based on the number of hits. For each hit/attack you actually do on enemy, your score will increase by 1 point
  • You get 5 points for each attack you land on the enemy
  • HAVE FUN and ENJOY THE GAME
